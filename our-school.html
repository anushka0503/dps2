Our School

The Delhi Public School, Fatehpur is a recognized School up to VIIIth class and affiliated with Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi. It is an ISO 9001:2015 certified school. It Functions under the aegis of the DPS educational and Cultural Society registered under Societies Registration Act, 1860. The School was established in 0000 with a limited number of children . In the course of time it grew from strength to strength and in 1984 the school shifted to its permanent accommodation in New Project (NP) area. It’s Kindergarten (KG) Wing, however, continues to be located in the Old Project. The School is one of the forerunner amongst the elite education institution in city .This has been possible due to candid effort, clairvoyant vision and keen interest of the School staff. Today , our School boasts of being a pioneer in the sphere of modern education and well equipped Computer Labs and Smart Classes to develop the academic veracity of the students, but also provide the best possible sport and recreation facilities and encourage extra-curricular activities with the aim of developing all round talents amongst the students. In our word, our school is a ‘Model School’ with its sole goal to make each individual student of today, perfect students of tomorrow.

OUR VISION

DELHI PUBLIC SCHOOL FATEHPUR is a school with a difference. By stressing on the dignity and right of children, by educating them to meet the contemporary needs, the school endeavors to produce young people having grace, joy and balance. While emphasizing on interesting and creative learning process, the school aims at making the desire to gain knowledge, an exciting journey in discovery. The school caters to the educational needs of the children of all sections of society. The academic system is structured to maintain high academic standards through a team of well qualified teachers. The numbers are kept at manageable proportions to cater for individual attention and care.

OUR MISSION

DELHI PUBLIC SCHOOL FATEHPUR is ambitiously determined to provide wide encompassing education thus widening a child’s horizons and kindling wide ranging interest to mould a child’s development into a responsible mature citizen for the nation. The school’s philosophy is that while imparting education, a concrete efforts should be made for character building by inculcating value of integrity, self-discipline, secularism, tolerance, respect for individual right compassion for under privileged and dedication to the nation.To develop our students into responsible, harmonious and integrated individuals through ever evolving curricular and co-curricular activities.
